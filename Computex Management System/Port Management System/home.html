SPONSOR A BRIGHT PROGRAM

Education is the bridge that links us from our state of unknowing to the state of knowing. Mentorship is the guide that takes us from a state of knowing, to a state of being able to impliment our knowing in a manner that benefits and makes us better people in the society. This is however not accessible to all despite it being a basic need. More and more children and families live in poverty of both financial will power and information access. We aim to bridge this gap, and we invite you to help. Sponsor a bright is an organization running scholarship funds for the needy in the society. It offers a platform where donors can access and sponsor accademically, needy pupils and students in the remotest regions in Kenya.
